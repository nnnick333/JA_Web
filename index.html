WHO ARE WE?

Retter Toys is a student run Junior Achievement Company Program based in Toronto. We are composed of 26 students, seven of which are a VPs of a variety of different departments and are led by our president. We sell stuffed animals that allows consumers to heat in a microwave or chilled in a freezer to retain their desired temperature. The products are filled with organic rice and infused with a lavender scent in order to maximize comfort for all ages.

Our main focus is to give back which sparked our initiative to give a portion of proceeds towards Sober Creek Wildlife Center.

GET INVOLVED

At Retter Toys, we have a dream to stop the environmental degradation of our planet and to build a future where humans and nature coexist harmoniously. As a part of our commitment, Retter Toys has partnered with Soper Creek Wildlife Rescue to support animal conservation.

Retter Toys focuses on saving endangered animals one warm cuddle at a time. In an effort to address the rapid increase of endangered animals, Retter Toys has launched a line of stuffed animals that offers heating and cooling therapy.

By purchasing a stuffed animal, not only will you have a furry friend to cuddle with, but you will also be supporting this initiative. A portion of the proceeds will be donated to Soper Creek, where it will go towards preserving and sustaining the health of indigenous animals and providing wildlife education to youth.

Retter Toys' line of stuffed animals ​ retail for $15.00 CAD, 28% made from each toy sold will be donated to Soper Creek Wildlife Rescue
