AFFILIATION WITH SOPER CREEK WILDLIFE

​"In the end, we will only conserve what we love; we will only love what we understand & we will understand what we have been taught" -Baba Dioum


​Soper Creek Wildlife Rescue is one of Durham regions only wildlife restoration, rehabilitation and preservation organizations. Their priority is to improve the health and welfare of the animals that come under their care, as well as to educate youth about the importance of human-nature coexistence. In the end, they hope to alleviate the suffering inflicted upon animals - most of the times by humans.


​At Soper Creek, they believe that all wildlife species should be treated equally. Each of them plays an important role in the ecosystem and deserve a chance to recover and be released back into their natural habitat, whether overpopulated or threatened, hit by a car or injured by a domestic pet.


​Without donations, Soper Creek Wildlife Rescue would not be sustainable. Caring for sick, injured and orphaned wildlife requires adequate resources, as well as the collaboration of rehabilitation, volunteers and passionate citizens. Donations are directed towards essential supplies such as medication, veterinary care, housing, specialty foods, and more.


AFFILIATION WITH SOPER CREEK WILDLIFE

​At Retter Toys, we have a dream to stop the environmental degradation of our planet and to build a future where humans and nature coexist harmoniously. As a part of our commitment, Retter Toys has partnered with Soper Creek Wildlife Rescue to support animal conservation.

​By purchasing a stuffed animal, not only will you have a furry friend to cuddle with, but you will also be supporting this initiative. A portion of the proceeds will be donated to Soper Creek, where it will go towards preserving and sustaining the health of indigenous animals and providing wildlife education to youth.
