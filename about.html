ABOUT US

OUR MISSION

Our focus is to save endangered animals one cuddle at a time.


HOW DO THESE ANIMALS WORK?

Retter Toys offers a line of lavender scented stuffed animals that is each filled with organic rice. Homemade, these animals each represent an animal that needs our help!

Microwave your stuffed friend for one and a half minutes for a warm cuddle or cool it down in the refrigerator for a cooling pack!


AFFILIATION WITH SOPER CREEK WILDLIFE

​At Retter Toys, we have a dream to stop the environmental degradation of our planet and to build a future where humans and nature coexist harmoniously. As a part of our commitment, Retter Toys has partnered with Soper Creek Wildlife Rescue to support animal conservation.

By purchasing a stuffed animal, not only will you have a furry friend to cuddle with, but you will also be supporting this initiative. A portion of the proceeds will be donated to Soper Creek, where it will go towards preserving and sustaining the health of indigenous animals and providing wildlife education to youth.

​Retter Toys is a company formed from the non-profit organization, Junior Achievement (JA). Junior Achievement is a worldwide company made to give students advice, insight and education about business, finance and entrepreneurship. Our team consists of 26 students and 6 advisors, who all work in an 18-week program in creating a company of our own.


The 26 of us are separated into 7 departments: Marketing, Human Resources, CSR, Finance, Sales and Technology. There is an executive for each group and a president of the whole company.


After several weeks of brainstorming and idea pitching, we have decided and produced our company and product, Retter Toys. Retter Toys offers a line of heating and cooling packs in the form of adorable, cuddly, stuffed animals.


Retter Toys is heavily invested in the cause behind its product, which is bringing awareness to endangered animals, such as Piper the Panda, who represent pandas facing who are extinction as of now.


Our slogan, “Embrace the Change”, reflects Retter Toys' company values while it signifies how the use of our product will create change and bring awareness to a large number of endangered animals, through something as simple as an embrace.
